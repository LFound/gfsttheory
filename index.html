Gravitational Field State Theory

By an independent researcher (2025)

Gravitational Field State Theory (GFST) proposes that gravity behaves not as a uniform force but as a dynamic field with distinct states — tight, diffuse, and transitional — depending on the local mass and energy density. This reinterpretation of gravity explains a range of cosmic anomalies currently attributed to dark matter, without requiring exotic or undetected particles.
