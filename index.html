Gravitational Field State Theory

Independent publication (2025)

Gravitational Field State Theory (GFST) proposes that gravity behaves not as a uniform force but as a dynamic field with distinct states ("tight", "diffuse" and "transitional") depending on local mass and energy density. This reinterpretation of gravity is part of a broader conceptual framework known as Field Tension Physics; the study of elastic field behaviour, gravitational resistance and transitional state mechanics.
